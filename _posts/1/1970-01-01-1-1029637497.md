# Android MITM Cert

```sh
cp mitm.pem `openssl x509 -inform PEM -subject_hash_old -noout -in mitm.pem`.0

mount -o rw,remount /system

mv abcdef00.0 /system/etc/security/cacerts/

chmod 644 /system/etc/security/cacerts/abcdef00.0.0
```
